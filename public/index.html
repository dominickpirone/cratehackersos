Import your members from Kartra

Active/canceled membership lives in Kartra (Stripe & PayPal only process payments), and Kartra's API can't list contacts in bulk — so export a CSV and upload it. Once per audience.

  1. In Kartra: Contacts → All contacts
  2. Advanced filters → pick a group (e.g. your active member tag)
  3. Select-all → Export to CSV
  4. Upload below — name it members, cancellations, etc.

Level 11 Funnel

Live split-test performance for lander.cratehackers.com/level11 — visitors → checkout clicks → conversions → revenue, per variant.

Funnel
From
To

By variant — winner = highest earnings per visitor (EPC)

Conversions by tier

Visitors + checkout clicks are tracked by the lander pixel. Conversions + revenue are pulled live from your Kartra sales ledger — Kartra’s thank-you redirect bypasses the lander, so the pixel can’t see the sale. Totals are exact; per-variant conversions are estimated from each variant’s checkout-click share (the variant a buyer saw isn’t recorded at the off-site Kartra checkout). Tier is inferred from amount charged — ~$99 monthly · ~$891 annual · ≥$1,200 lifetime.
